Here are some of our most frequently asked questions.

Is this template only for agencies?

Not at all. While built with agencies in mind, it's also perfect for freelancers, consultants, creatives, and solo business owners. Whether you're showcasing services or personal work, it’s flexible enough to fit a wide range of professional needs.

Can I delete sections I don’t need?

Yes, absolutely. Every section in the template is modular, so you can easily remove or rearrange anything you don’t need. Whether you're simplifying the layout or tailoring it to your brand, editing is fast and fully no-code inside Framer.

Will the template be responsive on all devices?

Yes, the template is fully responsive, ensuring your site looks great on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

How quickly can I get my website up and running?

With this template, you can have a professional-looking website live within just minutes! – no prior experience required!
